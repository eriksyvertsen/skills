USVC Brand
Design Guidelines

Back the companies building the future. Before it's obvious.

A comprehensive brand system designed for US Venture Capital—a fund by AngelList Asset Management that broadens access to venture capital for retail investors.

Brand Overview

USVC represents a fundamental shift in venture capital access. We're not just offering exposure to venture—we're democratizing early-stage investment for retail investors who believe in backing the future.

Mission

Broaden access to venture capital and empower everyday investors to participate in the companies building tomorrow's world.

Positioning

Institutional-grade venture investing, made accessible. We combine financial credibility with modern, tech-forward design to challenge VC exclusivity.

Tone of Voice

Direct — we say what we mean. Authoritative — we know venture. Accessible — we explain complex concepts simply. Contrarian — we question the status quo.

Visual Identity Principles

Sharp Corners Everywhere

0px border-radius on buttons, cards, inputs. This is not accidental—it's a defining visual characteristic that conveys precision and institutional rigor.

Generous Whitespace

Vertical padding between sections reaches 7.2rem. Wide container padding breathes. Whitespace is not emptiness—it's clarity.

Serif & Sans Pairing

Romie (serif) for display establishes financial authority. Sohne (sans-serif) for UI conveys modern, tech-forward efficiency. Together they speak trust and innovation.

Minimal Decoration

No gradients on UI elements. No drop shadows. No unnecessary ornamentation. Clean line work and deliberate color create sophistication without clutter.

Logo & Wordmark

The USVC wordmark is set in a heavy sans-serif (Sohne), all caps, with tight negative letter-spacing. The logo should feel condensed, modern, and unmistakably institutional.

Logo Specifications

Specification Value Notes
Font Family Sohne Bold Commercial font. Use Inter Bold as fallback in web.
Letter Spacing -8% (tight) Compressed feel creates premium, condensed appearance.
Primary Color #00172E (Navy) Default dark logo on light backgrounds.
Secondary Color #FAFDFF (Off-White) Inverse version for dark backgrounds.
Minimum Size 120px width Below this size, legibility suffers. Never use below 80px.
Clear Space 1/3 of logo height Maintain minimum clear space on all sides.

Logo Do's & Don'ts

Use Black or Navy

Navy (#00172E) on light backgrounds, Off-White (#FAFDFF) on dark backgrounds. Maintain high contrast.

Don't Use Colors

The logo should never be rendered in brand blue or other accent colors. Monochrome only.

Maintain Clear Space

Always provide breathing room around the logo. Use at least 1/3 of the logo height as clear space.

Don't Distort

Never stretch, skew, or rotate the logo. Don't add shadows, outlines, or effects.

Honor Tight Letter-Spacing

The compressed letter-spacing is integral to the brand. Never expand or adjust the spacing.

Don't Separate Letters

The logo must always appear as a unified wordmark. Never break it into individual letters or rearrange them.

Typography

The USVC typographic system pairs a distinctive serif for display with a modern sans-serif for UI and body text. This combination balances institutional authority with contemporary accessibility.

Font Families

Display & Headings

Primary: Romie (serif)

Fallback: DM Serif Display, Playfair Display

Weight: 500

Letter-spacing: -3% on large (H1-H2), -1.5% on smaller (H3-H4)

Romie's distinctive proportions and serifs communicate premium positioning and financial credibility. The tight negative letter-spacing creates a compressed, confident voice.

Body & UI Text

Primary: Sohne (sans-serif)

Fallback: Inter

Weight: 400 (body), 500-700 (emphasis)

Letter-spacing: -0.03em

Sohne's geometric precision and open letterforms ensure clarity at all sizes. Slight negative letter-spacing improves flow without sacrificing readability.

Layout Guidelines

Container Width

Max-width: 1200px

Padding: 6rem horizontal on desktop, 3rem on tablet, 1.5rem on mobile

Content feels spacious without becoming difficult to scan. Optimal line length (55-75 characters) is maintained for readability.

Section Padding

Vertical: 7.2rem (3XL) between major sections

Internal: 3rem (XL) between component groups

Alternating background colors create visual rhythm and section delineation.

Component Spacing

Between elements: 1.5rem - 3rem

Button groups: 1rem gap

Card padding: 2rem internal padding

Consistent spacing creates visual cohesion and predictable interaction patterns.

Grid System

Columns: Responsive (2-4 on desktop, 1-2 on tablet, 1 on mobile)

Gap: 3rem (XL)

Auto-fit grid columns prevent awkward layouts at various breakpoints.

Buttons & Interactive Elements

Buttons are primary interaction points. The USVC button system is intentional, consistent, and immediately recognizable across all applications.

Button Variants

Primary Button

Navy background, off-white text. Used for primary CTAs and main actions.

Blue CTA Button

Bright blue background. Used for secondary actions, links, and interactive emphasis.

Ghost Button

Transparent background with navy border. Used for secondary actions on light backgrounds.

Button Key Specifications
  • Border Radius: 0px (sharp corners)
  • Font Family: Sohne (Inter fallback)
  • Font Weight: 500
  • Padding: 1rem 2rem (standard), adjust for size variants
  • Transition: 0.3s all ease
  • Text Transform: None (maintain original case)
  • Letter Spacing: -0.03em

Interactive States

State Style Use Case
Default As specified in variant Initial button appearance
Hover Darken background by 1-2 shades, maintain border User indicates intent to interact
Active / Pressed Darker background, no outline change Button is being clicked/held
Focus (Keyboard) 2px outline in primary blue Keyboard navigation accessibility
Disabled 40-60% opacity, cursor: not-allowed Action unavailable in current context

Input Fields

Input fields follow the same design language: sharp corners, navy borders on focus, generous padding.

Photography & Imagery Style

Visual content in USVC materials should convey institutional credibility with modern, tech-forward energy. Photography is clean, purposeful, and emotionally intelligent.

Subject Matter
  • Diverse founders and teams (authentic, not staged)
  • Product moments and workspace energy
  • Data visualizations and abstract concepts
  • Investor profiles and community moments
  • Company milestones and growth moments
Visual Treatment
  • Sharp focus, minimal depth of field blur
  • Neutral to cool color temperature
  • High contrast between subject and background
  • Daylight or clean, institutional lighting
  • No heavy filters or stylization
Composition
  • Rule of thirds or centered composition
  • Plenty of negative space
  • Clean, uncluttered backgrounds
  • Strong visual hierarchy
  • No busy or distracting elements
Dark/Light Backgrounds
  • Dark navy sections: light/bright photography
  • Light sections: can support darker, moodier shots
  • Always maintain 3:1+ contrast for readability
  • Overlay with semi-transparent navy when needed
  • Test text legibility over all images

Design System Applications

The USVC design system extends beyond digital. Here's how to apply these principles to decks, marketing collateral, and extended brand touchpoints.

Presentation Deck Template

Key Guidelines
  • Cover Slide: Navy background, off-white USVC logo, large serif headline, tagline in light blue
  • Content Slides: Alternate between off-white and ice-blue backgrounds every 2-3 slides
  • Section Dividers: Full navy slide with off-white section title, no other content
  • Typography: Serif headlines (DM Serif Display), sans body (Inter), tight negative letter-spacing
  • Button/CTA: Navy or primary blue, sharp corners, generous padding
  • Spacing: 7.2rem between major sections, 3rem between points, 1.5rem between list items
  • Data Slides: Tables with navy headers, blue accent lines, light gray alternate rows
  • Master Slide: Subtle page border (light pink #FFE8E0) frames entire deck

Email Template

Email Header & Footer
  • Header: Navy background, off-white USVC logo left-aligned, 60px height
  • Body: Off-white background, serif headlines, sans body text
  • CTA Buttons: Primary blue or navy, sharp corners, 200px max width, centered
  • Footer: Light gray background, small text (#798594), legal links in navy
  • Dividers: Warm gray (#EFF2F4) 1px horizontal lines between sections
  • Max Width: 600px (optimal for email clients)
